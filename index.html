Timothy Merlis

I am an Associate Professor in Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences at McGill University and a Canada Research Chair (Tier 2) in Atmospheric and Climate Dynamics. I am also a member of the McGill Space Institute.

My research is focused on climate dynamics including tropical meteorology, climate sensitivity, the ocean-atmosphere general circulation, and the atmospheric hydrological cycle. I aim to expose the physical mechanisms underlying changes in both past and future climates.
